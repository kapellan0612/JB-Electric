# JB Electric — Design System

## Overview

JB Electric is a residential and commercial electrical contractor serving the greater Massachusetts area. The brand is built around trust, safety, and high-visibility energy — communicated through a bold navy-and-gold palette, a lightning-bolt logo mark, and confident, direct copy.

## Services
Four core service lines — always lead with these in marketing collateral:
1. **Electrical Installations** — New construction, additions, upgrades. Panels, outlets, fixtures, code compliance.
2. **Lighting Solutions** — Indoor, outdoor, landscape, commercial. LED retrofits and smart lighting systems.
3. **Wiring & Rewiring** — Knob-and-tube replacement, circuit additions, service upgrades, troubleshooting.
4. **Electrical Repairs** — Diagnostics, outlet replacements, breaker issues, emergency callouts.

## CONTENT FUNDAMENTALS

### Voice & Tone
- **Direct and confident.** JB Electric does not hedge. Copy leads with capability, not fluff.
- **Professional but approachable.** Not corporate — this is a local trade business. Warm, reliable, no-nonsense.
- **Action-oriented.** CTAs are imperative: "Call Today," "Get a Free Quote," "Schedule Service."
- **Safety-aware.** Trust language (insured, licensed) is always present — never buried.

### Casing
- Headlines: **Title Case** (e.g., "Wired for Excellence!")
- Service names: Title Case
- Contact info (email): ALL CAPS (e.g., `JHORNETBC@GMAIL.COM`)
- Body copy: Sentence case

### Phone Number Format
Always written with **period separators**: `978.397.9878` — never dashes or parentheses.

### Pronouns
Second-person "you/your" for customer-facing copy. First-person "we/our" for trust statements ("We're fully insured").

### Emoji & Special Characters
- **No emoji** in formal collateral.
- **Yellow checkmarks** (✓ or checkbox icons) are the signature list marker — always used with service lists.
- The lightning bolt is a brand graphic element, not a text character.

### Examples of Brand Copy
- "Wired for Excellence!"
- "Electrical Installations · Lighting Solutions · Wiring & Rewiring · Electrical Repairs"
- "Fully Insured · Licensed · Free Quotes"
- "Residential / Commercial"

## VISUAL FOUNDATIONS

### Color System
Three blues (trust, depth, stability) + three golds (energy, action, urgency).

| Role | Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | Deep Navy | `#0E2A5C` |
| Secondary | Brand Blue | `#1B3D8F` |
| Tertiary | Electric Blue | `#2E5AAA` |
| Accent | Bolt Yellow | `#FFD00E` |
| Midtone | Bolt Amber | `#F7A823` |
| Deep Accent | Bolt Orange | `#F26A21` |
| White | — | `#FFFFFF` |
| Light BG | — | `#F4F6FB` |

**Usage rules:**
- Navy (`#0E2A5C`) for wordmarks, headings, body text
- Brand Blue (`#1B3D8F`) for backgrounds, panels, large blocks
- Bolt Yellow (`#FFD00E`) for CTAs, checkmarks, highlights — always on navy for contrast
- Bolt Orange (`#F26A21`) only for alerts and emergency emphasis
- Never put yellow text on white — insufficient contrast

### Typography
- **Display / Headlines:** Archivo (geometric grotesque, strong italics echo the slanted wordmark). Bold and Black weights for headlines. Google Fonts substitute used — flag for replacement if brand has licensed files.
- **Body / UI:** Manrope (open, round, highly legible). Regular and SemiBold for body copy.

**Type Scale:**
- Display: 48–72px, Archivo Black/Bold
- H1: 36px, Archivo Bold
- H2: 28px, Archivo Bold
- H3: 22px, Archivo SemiBold
- Body: 16px, Manrope Regular
- Small/Caption: 13px, Manrope Regular
- Label/Tag: 11px, Manrope SemiBold, uppercase tracked

### Logo
- **Mark:** "JB" letters in Brand Blue gradient with a Bolt Yellow lightning bolt through the junction
- **Wordmark:** "ELECTRIC" in Deep Navy, wide-tracked, bold
- **Clear space:** Minimum 1× the "E" cap-height on all sides
- **On dark backgrounds:** Use full-color logo (blues + yellow bolt remain legible)
- **On light backgrounds:** Use full-color logo
- **Never:** Stretch, rotate, recolor, or place on busy backgrounds

### Layout & Composition
- **Diagonal bolt motif:** The lightning bolt slash appears as a dividing element between panels (seen on business card back — white left panel / navy right panel with yellow bolt slash divider)
- **Two-panel layouts:** Light (white/light gray) on left, Navy on right — signature layout
- **Full-bleed navy panels** for marketing blocks and hero sections
- **No decorative gradients** as backgrounds — gradients are reserved for the bolt icon only

### Backgrounds
- White or near-white (`#F4F6FB`) for content areas
- Deep Navy (`#0E2A5C`) or Brand Blue (`#1B3D8F`) for feature sections
- Subtle diagonal or bolt-shaped dividers between sections
- No photography in the brand kit currently — bolt graphic + color panels carry visual weight

### Cards & Containers
- Light card: white background, subtle box-shadow (`0 2px 8px rgba(14,42,92,0.10)`), border-radius `8px`
- Dark card: Navy background, yellow accent border or top stripe
- No colored left-border-only cards
- Border: `1px solid rgba(14,42,92,0.12)` for light mode dividers

### Shadows & Elevation
- Level 1 (cards): `0 2px 8px rgba(14,42,92,0.10)`
- Level 2 (dropdowns/modals): `0 8px 24px rgba(14,42,92,0.18)`
- No colored shadows

### Corner Radii
- Buttons: `6px`
- Cards: `8px`
- Badges/tags: `4px`
- Pill/full-round: `999px` (used for phone number display badges)

### Spacing System
Base unit: `8px`. Scale: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128px.

### Animations
- No brand-specified animation guidelines
- Recommended: subtle fade-in on scroll, fast transitions (150–200ms ease-out)
- Hover on buttons: slightly darker shade or `opacity: 0.9`
- Press state: `scale(0.97)` + slight darken

### Iconography
See ICONOGRAPHY section below.

### Imagery
- No stock photography in current brand kit
- Bold graphic/illustrative approach: color panels, lightning bolt motifs
- If photography is used: high-contrast, warm-to-neutral color grade; no cold/desaturated imagery
